Call of Duty: Modern Warfare
"Call of Duty: Modern Warfare", released in 2019, is a first-person shooter developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. It's a reboot of the original Modern Warfare series and features a modern-day setting with a focus on intense, realistic combat scenarios. The game includes a single-player campaign, multiplayer modes, and a cooperative Special Ops mode.
Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II
Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II (2022) is a first-person shooter developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. It's the sequel to the 2019 Modern Warfare reboot and the 19th installment in the overall Call of Duty series. The game features a global campaign, immersive multiplayer combat, and an evolved Special Ops mode with tactical co-op gameplay.
Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III
Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III is a 2023 first-person shooter game developed by Sledgehammer Games and published by Activision. It is the twentieth installment of the Call of Duty series and is the third entry in the rebooted Modern Warfare sub-series, following Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II.
